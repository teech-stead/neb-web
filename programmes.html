Programmes

nEbO, launched 7 July 2007, is the junior membership arm of the Labour Movement (NTUC). It is a lifestyle club that actively reaches out to youth aged 12-25, socially engaging them in creating a vibrant community that is Work-Ready, World-Ready and Life-Ready.

Members are given the opportunity to hone their leadership skills and develop their potential through various events and initiatives under the communities of Care, Entrepreneurship, Lifestyle, Music, Sports and Enrichment. At nEbO, nEbOians get to meet other like-minded youth who share the same interests and participate in activities they love together.

Youth Leaders who are identified at nEbO events are also entitled to attend nEbO's signature LEAD (Learn, Engage and Develop) Programme. This programme strives to nurture and develop their leadership capabilities and skill sets within their areas of interest. The other leadership programme tailored just for Secondary School students is LEAD (Learn, Engage and Develop) Junior which includes specially crafted leadership modules targeted at the younger group to prepare them to be Work-Ready, World-Ready and Life-Ready.

What’s more, nEbO also offers a membership programme that opens the door to a host of exciting benefits and privileges under both the nEbO and Plus! Membership programme. Members get to enjoy discounted movie tickets, special admission rates to Gardens by the Bay, Wavehouse Sentosa and many more!
