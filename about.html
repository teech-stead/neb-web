About nEbO

Launched 7 July 2007, nEbO (stands for nobody Enjoys being Ordinary) is the junior membership arm of the Labour Movement (NTUC) in Singapore.

Because You’re Only Young Once

Launched 7 July 2007, nEbO (stands for nobody Enjoys being Ordinary) is the junior membership arm of the Labour Movement (NTUC) in Singapore. It is a lifestyle club that actively reaches out to youths aged between 12 and 25, socially engaging them by creating a vibrant community that guides and cultivates them to be Work-Ready, World-Ready and Life-Ready.

Because Your Future Matters

In efforts to get nEbOians to be work-ready, a unique “Headstart” program has been designed to inspire youths through holistic work exposure and upskilling programmes while enabling them to discover their passion and future career path. There are four main types of programme under Headstart:

DISCOVERY

Learning journeys

EXPERIENCE

Internships

ADVICE & CONNECTIONS

Networking sessions with industry leaders

KNOWLEDGE

Targeted workshops

Because You’re One of Us

Besides meeting other like-minded young people who share similar interests and to participate in activities they enjoy together, nEbOians can also enjoy attractive benefits and privileges under both nEbO and Link Rewards Programme. Members can look forward to enjoying exclusive discounted offers at Gong Cha, KF1 Karting, Trapped Escape Room and many more!
