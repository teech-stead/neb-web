Renewal

Continue being a Valued nEbOian! Renew your nEbO membership at an affordable rate today! Applicable for all nEbO members whose membership have expired/are expiring.

Renew your nEbO membership now
  • 1 Log in with your credentials - the last 3 digits and letter of your NRIC and Date of Birth (e.g. 123ADDMMYYYY)
  • 2 If you have issues logging in, email us ata nEbOcso2@nEbO.com.sg.
  • 3 *In alignment with the new guidelines from the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC), we will no longer be using NRIC/FIN numbers for login to the nEbO member portal.
  • 4 Click on the "Renew Membership" button to proceed to the renewal page
  • 5 Click "Submit" at the bottom of the renewal page
  • 6 Complete your online payment to renew your membership
